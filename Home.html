Peebles
Founded in 1152
Contra Nando Incrementum

Peebles is a picturesque town situated in the heart of the Scottish Borders. With a population of just over 9000 people and a scenic location, many tourists come from around the world to experience its small town charm. With festivals such as Beltane, and the Rolling Hills of the Borders surrounding the town, it is a blossoming town with much to offer.
