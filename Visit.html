Visit Peebles

With many places to visit, perhaps the most popular is Glentress, a mountain biking wonderland, which hosted part of the UCI competition in 2023, visited by the Prince of Monaco himself who stayed in a nearby hotel.

There are many other fantastic places to visit in Peebles itself, including the Parish church...

...and many different pubs to choose from. If you are looking for a place to stay, the Tontine is a famous building from 1808 and is widely regarded as a top notch hotel.

Another fantastic location if you fancy travelling a couple miles outside of town is Traquair House, the oldest inhabited house in Scotland. Fun for all the family, you can explore much of the old house, visited by 27 Scottish Kings and Queens and even try a go at the escape tunnel which runs down the left side of the house. A historical fact about Traquair is that, due to their family lineage of Stuart's, the Bear Gates, the gates at the end of the garden which are by the entrance of the visitor attraction, have not been opened since the last Stuart sat upon the throne of Scotland, and will remain closed unless another Stuart sits upon the throne.
